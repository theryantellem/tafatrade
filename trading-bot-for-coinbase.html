What Is Binance?

Binance is a newer exchange that has achieved a high degree of popularity over its short existence. It is one of the top crypto exchanges for trading Bitcoin, and also has a large range of altcoins available for trading. It is known for its low trading fees and low spreads, making it an especially popular exchange for high-activity and algorithmic crypto traders.
